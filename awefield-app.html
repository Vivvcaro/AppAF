choose category

What do you need today?

Each category opens onto three depths. Tap more to read what each holds.

Arrival
For grounding, softness, and nervous system regulation.
more
Arrival is the doorway. Sessions in this category bring you into the body, slow the breath, and signal to the nervous system that it is safe to settle. Use this when you arrive home tense, before sleep, or before any deeper work. The acoustic profile is typically low frequency, sparse, with long durations between events.
Healing
For emotional processing, release, and inner repair.
more
Healing sessions hold space for what has been waiting. They invite the body to soften and the heart to open. Tears are common. Many sessions in this category feature human voice, breath, or instruments that resemble the human voice. Use this when you sense something that needs to move through you.
Expansion
For clarity, creativity, exploration, and altered awareness.
more
Expansion sessions widen the field of attention beyond the immediate self. Visions, fractal imagery, archetypes, and shifts in time perception are common. The acoustic profile leans toward spatial, harmonically rich, and dynamically wide. Use these when you have time, energy, and a settled mind.

How deep do you want to go?

The depth defines the duration, the acoustic intensity, and how far the session opens.

Gentle
A soft entry — 10 to 18 minutes. Recommended for first-time listeners.
Deep
A longer descent — 20 to 35 minutes. Headphones strongly recommended.
Immersive
Full-length, full-field — 40 to 60 minutes. Best in low light without interruption.
